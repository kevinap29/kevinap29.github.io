# The Challenge
The energy sector requires a balance between professional corporate identity and accessible technical information. PT. GJE's previous presence was limited, making it difficult for potential partners to understand the full scope of their energy solutions and project history.

# Technical Implementation
I chose **Laravel** for its elegant syntax and robust security features, which are critical for a corporate website. The frontend was built using **Bootstrap** to ensure a mobile-first, responsive experience across all devices.

### Key Architectural Decisions:
- **Blade Templating**: Used for modular and reusable UI components.
- **Eloquent ORM**: Efficiently managed project portfolios and service listings.
- **SEO Optimization**: Implemented clean URLs and meta tag management to improve search engine visibility.

# Key Features
- **Dynamic Portfolio**: A searchable gallery of completed energy projects.
- **Service Modules**: Detailed pages for each energy sector (Renewables, Oil & Gas, etc.).
- **Responsive Admin Panel**: Allows the client to update content without technical knowledge.

# Final Outcome
The new website led to a significant increase in lead inquiries and provided a professional platform for the company to secure new international contracts.
