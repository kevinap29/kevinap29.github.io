---
title: 'Launched Top Hashtags on Mobile with .NET MAUI'
---

To expand the reach of the Top Hashtags API, I developed a native mobile application using **.NET MAUI** and **C#**. The cross-platform framework allowed me to target both Android and iOS from a single codebase. I focused on building a clean, responsive UI with **MudBlazor** to ensure a smooth user experience when researching trending hashtags.
